# 🏠 Koreans Next Door (KND)

## What is KND?

Koreans Next Door is a **community hospitality group** started by friends from Sigwang Church (Seoul). The mission: walk alongside internationals living in Korea so they feel at home in a foreign land.

- **Not a formal organization** — a group of locals from church
- **Not a religious program** — events are open to all faiths, backgrounds, walks of life
- **No hidden agendas** — just genuine hospitality
- Tagline: *"Come as a guest, stay as a neighbor."*

## Core Values
- Hospitality as the mechanism to make an unfamiliar city feel like home
- Welcoming the way they themselves wanted to be welcomed when living abroad
- Low-pressure, community-driven, figuring it out together

## Target Audience
Internationals living in or visiting Korea — expats, students, travelers — who feel the loneliness of not belonging.
